Bachelor of Science in Nursing

Develop the skills necessary to excel in the field of healthcare as a nurse, with a focus on clinical practice and patient care.

About the Program

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing program at Daeyang University prepares students for the nursing profession by providing hands-on clinical experience and theoretical knowledge in areas like patient care, anatomy, physiology, and pharmacology.

Program Structure

  • Year 1: Anatomy & Physiology, Microbiology, Nursing Ethics, Communication Skills
  • Year 2: Medical-Surgical Nursing, Nutrition, Pharmacology, Clinical Nursing Practice
  • Year 3: Pediatric Nursing, Psychiatric Nursing, Maternity Nursing, Community Health Nursing
  • Year 4: Advanced Clinical Practice, Research in Nursing, Leadership and Management in Nursing

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Community Health Nurse
  • Midwife
  • Nursing Administrator
  • Healthcare Consultant
