Daeyang School of Business and management sciences

Build your business acumen and leadership skills to succeed in today's fast-paced global market.

About the Programs

Daeyang School of Business and management sciences equips students with the tools and skills necessary to succeed in various sectors of the economy, including finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and management. The curriculum emphasizes both theory and practical applications, preparing students for leadership roles.

Program Offered

  • Bachelor of Commerce in Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Commerce in Accountancy
  • Bachelor of Commerce in Human Resource Management

Program Structure

  • Year 1: Principles of Management, Economics, Business Communication, Financial Accounting
  • Year 2: Marketing, Organizational Behavior, Corporate Finance, Business Law
  • Year 3: Entrepreneurship, Operations Management, International Business, Human Resource Management
  • Year 4: Strategic Management, Business Ethics, Project Management, Final Year Project

Careers

Graduates of the Business Administration program can pursue careers in the following areas:

  • Business Consultant
  • Marketing Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • Entrepreneur
  • Project Manager
  • Human Resources Manager
