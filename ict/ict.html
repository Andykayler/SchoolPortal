Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Equipping students with skills in software development, algorithms, data structures, and more to advance your career in the tech industry.

About the Program

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at Daeyang University offers an in-depth study of computer science fundamentals. This program prepares students to become problem solvers and leaders in the field of technology. Our curriculum focuses on software development, algorithms, database systems, artificial intelligence, and computer networking.

Program Structure

  • Year 1: Introduction to Programming, Calculus, Discrete Mathematics, Communication Skills
  • Year 2: Data Structures, Algorithms, Web Development, Computer Architecture
  • Year 3: Database Management Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Mobile Application Development
  • Year 4: Final Year Project, Advanced Algorithms, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning

Career Opportunities

    Graduates of the Computer Science program can pursue careers in the following areas:


  • Software Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • Artificial Intelligence Specialist
  • IT Consultant
